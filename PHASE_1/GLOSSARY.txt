C
Culture Points (CPx) - one of the Human's attribute, CPx increase happiness in the whole Base

G
Game - this game -> Post-Apstro Rescuer

K
Knowledge Points (KPx) - one of the Human's attribute, KPx is the "currency" which help update the Base and Spaceship

P
Player - Person, who play the Game

S
Spaceship - vehicle, which is controling by the Player

T
Technical Points (TPx) - one of the Human's attribute, TPx increase speed of doing the technical tasks
